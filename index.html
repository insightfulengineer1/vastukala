About Us

Vasstu o Nagar Tattwa is a Bhubaneswar-based architectural practice led by Architect Banisweta Patro. Blending traditional Odia vastu principles with modern urban aesthetics, we design homes, public spaces, and institutions that harmonize with culture, nature, and people.
